Discuss the CSS box model and its component. ans= The box model has four main components, from inside to outside. 1.content: This is the actual content of the element like text,images,videos. properties: height width its defines the size of the content area 2.padding is the space between the content and the border. properties: padding padding-top padding-right padding-bottom padding-left its increases space inside the element and padding area is transparent. 3.border: the border surrounds the padding and content. properties: border border-width border-style border-color its can be solid, dashed,dotted,dubble. 4.margin: Margin is the outermost space around the element. properties: margin margin-top margin-right margin-bottom margin-left its creates space between elements and margin is transparent.
